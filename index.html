The Cat Rescue Sanctuary

Welcome to our Cat Rescue website. Discover our mission and see how we bring a dignified life to abandoned cats across the country. We procure to bring world class treatment to every single feline that come to our Sanctuary. Browse this site in order to find a way o how you can help too.

We Cure

Well certified doctors and vets are working hard in our facilities to guarantee the good health of our cats. They save lives and we consider them the true heroes at Kai Center. Every new admission have a deep health check and they work alongside with the team with excellent hygiene standards.

We Care

Our team in the Sanctuary work very hard to deliver a world class caring service to the animals. They always bring healthy food and joyful games to make our cats happy and comfortable. Our mission is to treat every animal according to their special needs, both puppies and grown ups.

We Grow

Kai Center is growing every day. Thanks to small donations and crowdfunding campaigns we make this place not only bigger but also better. If you are interested in making a monetary contribution browse this site and you will find easy to contribute to our cause. Thank you in advance!

THE SANCTUARY

Our humble piece of Cat Heaven is located within a wild life area at the heart of the inner city. In this place our team and cats live in a natural environment. We make it easy for them to have a delightful life, and for you to come and visit us. Schools and families are welcome!

VOLUNTEER

As we continue to grow more help is needed to accomplish our goals. We have launched a variety of volunteering programs made for all ages and abilities. You can feed, pet, educate or simply learn from us in a wide range of possibilities. Just come and say Hi!
